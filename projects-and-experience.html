Risk Calculator

I applied and was hired to develop a website and server for the school of dentistry in Sheffield. I designed and implemented a stack to facilitate anonymous patient data being uploaded and a response to be given to the user to indicate the risk of admission of a disease. I have been developing a RESTful API and frontend for this project. I utilised test driven development for both the API and the front-end routing.

Pigeoniser

We are drowning in a world full of unclassified text due to lack of training data. In a world, where mass text classifiers require mass acquisition of accurate data, creating custom text classifiers does not scale. We wanted to create an idea that would crowd-source the creation of this training data, allowing any users regardless of ability or knowledge of classifiers to participate.
